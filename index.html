    ×

    Getting Started

    📝

    Word Search Pro is a fun word-finding game. Search for hidden words in the grid going in any direction: horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

    Find all the words before the timer runs out to win!

    Finding Words

    🔍

    To select a word, tap or click on the first letter and drag to the last letter. The selection must be in a straight line.

    When you find a word, it will be crossed off in the word list.

    Earning Coins

    🪙

    Earn coins by finding words, completing games, claiming daily rewards, and unlocking achievements.

    Use coins to buy themes, animations, power-ups, and more in the shop!

    Using Hints

    💡

    Stuck? Use the hint button to get help. Different hint types cost different amounts of coins.

    Use hints wisely to maximize your score!
